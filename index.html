Welcome to Relaxing Hot Springs

Open March 22 to October 28 for the 2019 Season

Pool Hours: 9:00am - 9:00pm

Come visit us at Relaxing Hot Springs. Whether you are an overnight visitor, or have arrived just for the day, there is an enjoyable activity for you. Spending the night in one of our cabins is a great way to experience the picturesque and serene environment of the Relaxing valley. The resort offers a restaurant, hot mineral-spring pools, gift shop and convenience store.

For hikers, there are several hiking trails through the old growth forest. A hike alongside the Relaxing River, to the Relaxing Falls is just a mile. Continue on the Lover's Lane Loop trail, for a 6 mile walk through the forest, ending back at Relaxing Hot Springs, where you started. Hike to Mink Lake, a 5 mile roundtrip hike with an elevation gain of 1450 feet.
